## ğŸ“ˆ Key Insights (KI)  

- **Highest patient volume:**  
  Gynecology records the highest visits due to strong outpatient demand.

- **Top revenue diagnosis:**  
  Viral infections generate the highest revenue from frequent and costly treatments.

- **Top-performing city:**  
  Mumbai leads in overall patient visits across all cities.

- **Demographic pattern:**  
  Females dominate Gynecology visits, and middle-aged groups show the highest visit frequency.

- **Doctor performance:**  
  A small group of doctors handle the majority of visits, indicating workload imbalance.

- **Seasonal trends:**  
  Patient visits peak during specific months aligned with seasonal illnesses.

- **Highest cost age group:**  
  Older adults incur the highest treatment costs due to complex medical conditions.

- **Preferred payment method:**  
  Credit Card is the most commonly used payment option.

- **Age vs cost relationship:**  
  Treatment cost shows a moderate positive correlation with patient age, with some outliers.
